# Steel Product Specifications Taxonomy

## 概述

完整的钢铁产品分类体系，用于关键词研究中的产品类型和规格词提取。

## 关键词 Triad 示例

### Rebar
- **Product**: Rebar, Deformed Bar, Steel Bar
- **Spec**: ASTM A615 Gr40/60, BS4449 Gr460, HRB400/500, 10mm/12mm/16mm
- **Usage**: construction, building, infrastructure, concrete reinforcement

**组合示例**：
- "ASTM A615 Gr60 rebar 12mm price"
- "BS4449 Gr460 rebar supplier Saudi Arabia"
- "HRB400 deformed bar for construction"

### HRC
- **Product**: Hot Rolled Coil, HRC, Steel Coil
- **Spec**: SPHC, SS400, Q235B, 2.0mm x 1250mm x C
- **Usage**: stamping, pipe making, structural

**组合示例**：
- "SPHC hot rolled coil 2mm supplier"
- "SS400 HRC price per ton Vietnam"
- "Q235B steel coil for pipe making"

### Seamless Pipe
- **Product**: Seamless Pipe, Seamless Steel Tube, SMLS
- **Spec**: ASTM A106 Gr.B, API 5L Gr.B, ST45.8, 2" SCH40
- **Usage**: oil and gas, boiler, high temperature

**组合示例**：
- "ASTM A106 Gr.B seamless pipe 2 inch"
- "API 5L Gr.B SMLS pipe supplier"
- "ST45.8 seamless tube for boiler"
