## Guest Post Pitch Templates

**适用场景**：向行业媒体/博客投稿

```

I'm [Your Name] from [Company], a steel manufacturer and exporter based in [Location].

This article would cover:
- Understanding steel grade designation systems
- Complete equivalence table for common grades (Rebar, HRC, Steel Pipe)
- How to select the right grade for specific applications
- Common mistakes when substituting grades

I believe this would be valuable for your audience because steel grade 
equivalents are frequently searched by engineers, procurement managers, 
and fabricators - and there's limited comprehensive resources available.

Here are some sample articles I've written:
- [Sample Link 1]
- [Sample Link 2]

The article would be approximately [1500-2000] words and include:
- Original research/data
- Comparison tables
- Practical examples

Let me know if you'd be interested. I'm happy to adjust the topic 
based on your editorial calendar.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Position]
[Company]
[Email]
[Website]
[LinkedIn Profile - optional]
```

## Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips

| Type | Good Examples | Bad Examples |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Guest Post | "Guest Post Pitch: [Specific Topic]" | "Guest Post" |
| Resource | "Resource Suggestion for [Page Name]" | "Link Request" |
| Broken Link | "Broken Link on [Page Name]" | "Your link is broken" |
| Follow-up | "Re: [Original Subject]" | "Following up again" |

### Email Timing

| Day | Best Time | Notes |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| Tuesday | 10 AM - 2 PM | Best overall |
| Wednesday | 10 AM - 2 PM | Second best |
| Thursday | 10 AM - 2 PM | Good |
| Monday | Avoid morning | People catching up |
| Friday | Avoid afternoon | People leaving early |

### Response Rate Optimization

**提高回复率**：
1. 个性化开头（提到对方网站具体内容）
2. 简洁明了（<200 词）
3. 明确价值（对方能得到什么）
4. 低压力 CTA（"Let me know if interested"）
5. 专业签名（包含职位、公司、网站）

**避免**：
- 群发痕迹（Dear Webmaster）
- 过长邮件（>300 词）
- 过度推销语气
- 多次催促
