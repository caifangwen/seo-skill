# [CATEGORY] Japanese Knives

## Category Overview

This category covers traditional and modern Japanese kitchen knives including Gyuto, Santoku, Nakiri, Deba, Yanagiba, and other specialized blades.
Products are sold wholesale to kitchenware retailers, restaurant supply stores, and culinary distributors.

## Writing Emphasis

- **Gyuto/Santoku**: stress blade steel (VG-10/Damascus/high carbon), edge retention, balance for professional kitchen use
- **Nakiri**: stress rectangular blade design, vegetable cutting precision, thin profile for clean cuts
- **Deba/Yanagiba**: stress traditional Japanese single-bevel design, fish butchering/sashimi slicing specialization
- **All products**: stress Japanese craftsmanship, steel quality, edge sharpness, wholesale/OEM customization options

## Tone Note

These are precision Japanese kitchen knives — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for the home chef") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
