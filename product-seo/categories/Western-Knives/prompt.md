# [CATEGORY] Western Knives

## Category Overview

This category covers Western-style kitchen knives including chef knives, paring knives, bread knives, carving knives, boning knives, and steak knives.
Products are sold wholesale to kitchenware retailers, restaurant supply stores, and culinary distributors.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Chef knives | chef knife | kitchen knife, cooking knife, Western chef knife |
| Paring knives | paring knife | fruit knife, utility knife, small kitchen knife |
| Bread knives | bread knife | serrated knife, bread slicer, kitchen bread knife |
| Carving knives | carving knife | slicing knife, meat slicer, roast carving knife |
| Boning & fillet knives | boning knife | fillet knife, meat deboning knife, flexible blade knife |
| Steak knives | steak knife | dinner knife, table knife, steak cutlery |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Chef knives**: stress all-purpose versatility, blade length (8-inch/10-inch), Western design, professional kitchen durability
- **Paring/utility knives**: stress precision work, peeling and detailed cutting, comfortable grip for extended use
- **Bread knives**: stress serrated edge design, clean slicing without crushing, crust penetration capability
- **Carving/boning knives**: stress blade flexibility, meat slicing precision, restaurant-grade performance
- **Steak knives**: stress table-ready sharpness, handle comfort, dishwasher-safe options for food service

## Tone Note

These are professional Western kitchen knives — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for home cooking") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
