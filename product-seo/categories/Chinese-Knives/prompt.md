# [CATEGORY] Chinese Knives

## Category Overview

This category covers traditional Chinese kitchen knives including cleavers, chopping knives, and vegetable slicers.
Products are sold wholesale to kitchenware retailers, restaurant supply stores, and Asian food service distributors.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Sub-type | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords |
|----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Chinese cleavers | Chinese cleaver | chopping knife, Chinese kitchen knife, vegetable cleaver |
| Meat cleavers | meat cleaver | bone cleaver, heavy cleaver, butcher cleaver |
| Vegetable slicers | vegetable cleaver | Chinese slicer, kitchen knife, vegetable knife |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Chinese cleavers**: stress versatile double-bevel design, blade height, multi-purpose use (poultry, meat, fish, vegetables)
- **Meat cleavers**: stress heavy-duty construction, bone-chopping capability, blade thickness, professional butcher grade
- **Vegetable cleavers**: stress lighter blade weight, precision slicing, thin cuts for vegetables and fruits
- **All products**: stress steel quality (high carbon/stainless), handle ergonomics, wholesale/OEM customization options

## Tone Note

These are professional Chinese kitchen knives — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for the home cook") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
