# [CATEGORY] Axes & Outdoor

## Category Overview

This category covers camping axes, hatchets, survival knives, and outdoor folding knives.
Products are sold wholesale to outdoor gear retailers, camping suppliers, and sporting goods stores.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Sub-type | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords |
|----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Camping axes / hatchets | camping axe | outdoor axe, splitting axe, tactical hatchet |
| Survival knives | survival knife | bushcraft knife, fixed blade knife, outdoor knife |
| Folding knives | folding knife | pocket knife, EDC knife, camping knife |
| Fixed blade knives | fixed blade knife | hunting knife, tactical knife, outdoor blade |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Axes/hatchets**: stress head material (forged steel), handle material (hickory/fiberglass), weight, balance for splitting/camping
- **Survival knives**: stress full tang construction, blade steel type, grip ergonomics, sheath quality for outdoor use
- **Folding knives**: stress locking mechanism (liner lock/frame lock), blade steel, portability for EDC
- **All products**: stress durability, outdoor performance, wholesale/bulk order availability

## Tone Note

These are rugged outdoor tools — keep descriptions practical and performance-focused.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for weekend adventurers") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
