# [CATEGORY] Pocket Knives

## Category Overview

This category covers folding pocket knives, EDC (everyday carry) knives, and small fixed blade knives.
Products are sold wholesale to outdoor gear retailers, EDC suppliers, and sporting goods stores.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Sub-type | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords |
|----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Folding knives | folding knife | pocket knife, EDC knife, everyday carry knife |
| Fixed blade knives | fixed blade knife | small hunting knife, neck knife, tactical knife |
| Tactical knives | tactical knife | tactical folding knife, tactical pocket knife |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Folding knives**: stress locking mechanism (liner lock/frame lock/back lock), blade steel (D2/VG-10/440C), portability for EDC
- **Fixed blade knives**: stress full tang construction, compact size, sheath quality for carry
- **All products**: stress blade sharpness, handle ergonomics (G10/aluminum/carbon fiber), pocket clip options, wholesale/bulk order availability

## Tone Note

These are functional EDC and outdoor tools — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for everyday adventures") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
