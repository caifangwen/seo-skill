# [CATEGORY] Cookware

## Category Overview

This category covers frying pans, sauté pans, stock pots, saucepans, woks, grills, and griddles.
Products are sold wholesale to kitchenware retailers, restaurant supply stores, and distributors.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Sub-type | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords |
|----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Frying pans / skillets | frying pan | sauté pan, skillet, nonstick pan |
| Stock pots / saucepans | stock pot | saucepan, cooking pot, stainless steel pot |
| Woks | wok | stir-fry wok, Chinese wok, carbon steel wok |
| Grills & griddles | grill pan | griddle, cooking grill, cast iron griddle |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Pans**: stress material (stainless steel/cast iron/nonstick), heat distribution, compatibility (induction/oven-safe), size range
- **Pots**: stress capacity (quarts/liters), lid type, pour spouts, handles (stay-cool), wholesale bulk options
- **Woks**: stress material (carbon steel/cast iron), flat vs round bottom, seasoning requirements, authentic stir-fry performance
- **Grills/griddles**: stress cooking surface, ridges for searing, even heat distribution, restaurant-grade durability

## Tone Note

These are professional cookware items — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for home cooking") — this is B2B wholesale copy for retailers and distributors.
