# [CATEGORY] Knife Accessories

## Category Overview

This category covers knife storage, sharpening tools, cutting boards, knife sheaths, and charcuterie boards.
Products are sold wholesale to kitchenware retailers and restaurant supply stores.

## Category Keywords (use naturally, don't stuff)

| Sub-type | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords |
|----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Magnetic holders / knife blocks | magnetic knife holder | knife storage, knife block, wall mount knife rack |
| Whetstones / sharpeners | whetstone knife sharpener | knife honing, sharpening stone, knife maintenance |
| Knife rolls / bags | knife roll bag | chef knife bag, knife storage roll, professional knife case |
| Cutting boards | cutting board | chopping board, bamboo cutting board, wooden cutting board |
| Knife sheaths / guards | knife sheath | blade guard, knife cover, protective knife sleeve |
| Charcuterie / serving boards | charcuterie board | serving board, cheese board, wooden serving platter |

## Writing Emphasis

- **Storage products**: stress capacity (how many knives), mounting method, material (bamboo/acacia/magnetic), safety
- **Sharpening products**: stress grit range, compatible blade types, ease of use for professional kitchens
- **Cutting boards**: stress surface material, juice grooves, NSF certification if applicable, size range
- **Sheaths/guards**: stress universal fit, blade protection, safe transport for chef kits
- **Serving boards**: stress aesthetic appeal, food-safe finish, wholesale customization options

## Tone Note

These are functional accessories — keep descriptions practical and specification-forward.
Avoid lifestyle language ("perfect for the home chef") — this is B2B wholesale copy.
