## SKILL-16: 采购建议总结

### 结构模板

```markdown
## Conclusion: which [产品] is better for [目标用户]

[总结主要差异]（1-2 句）.

[核心建议]（1-2 句）.

[价格说明]（可选）.

[最终建议 + CTA].
```

### 常用结论句式

```
- "The [特性] and [特性] are where [产品 A] proves to be better."
- "For most [应用场景], these differences won't be a major deciding factor."
- "However, the improved [特性] can make a big difference for users in [特定场景]."
- "From a total cost perspective, there isn't a noticeable difference over the lifecycle."
- "Overall, [产品] is a viable option for [应用场景]."
```

### 示例（法兰选型结论）

```markdown
## Conclusion: which flange type is better for your project

The pressure rating and temperature range are where material selection proves to be most critical. For most low-pressure water and air applications, carbon steel A105 flanges provide adequate performance at the lowest cost.

However, the superior corrosion resistance of stainless steel can make a big difference for users in chemical processing or marine environments. The elimination of maintenance and replacement costs often justifies the higher initial investment.

From a availability standpoint, both carbon steel and stainless steel flanges are readily available in standard sizes and ratings. Custom alloys or large diameter flanges may have longer lead times.

Overall, the right choice depends on your specific application requirements, environmental conditions, and budget constraints.
```
