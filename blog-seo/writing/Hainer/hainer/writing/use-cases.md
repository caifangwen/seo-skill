# SKILL-12: 使用场景描述

**用途**: 描述产品适用场景
**适用**: 所有文章类型

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## [产品名] is suitable for:

- **[场景 1]**: [详细说明].

- **[场景 2]**: [详细说明].

- **[场景 3]**: [详细说明].
```

---

## 常用场景分类

| 行业 | 典型应用 | 关键要求 |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Oil & Gas | 油气输送、炼油厂 | 高压、耐腐蚀、API 认证 |
| Petrochemical | 化工厂、石化装置 | 耐化学腐蚀、高温性能 |
| Water Treatment | 给水处理、污水处理 | 卫生级、耐氯腐蚀 |
| Power Generation | 火电、核电、水电 | 高温高压、可靠性 |
| Marine | 船舶管道、海洋平台 | 耐海水腐蚀、紧凑设计 |
| HVAC | 暖通空调系统 | 低压、成本效益 |
| Food & Beverage | 食品加工、酿酒 | 卫生级、易清洁 |
| Pharmaceutical | 制药厂、生物工程 | 无菌、高纯度 |

## 示例（不锈钢法兰）

```markdown
## Stainless Steel Flanges are suitable for:

- **Chemical Processing Plants**: The excellent corrosion resistance makes stainless steel flanges ideal for handling acids, alkalis, and other aggressive chemicals. They maintain integrity even when exposed to sulfuric acid, hydrochloric acid, and other corrosive substances.

- **Food and Beverage Industry**: Sanitary requirements demand materials that won't contaminate products. Stainless steel flanges meet FDA standards and can be easily cleaned and sterilized, making them perfect for food processing pipelines.

- **Marine Applications**: Saltwater environments cause rapid corrosion in carbon steel. Stainless steel flanges, especially 316 grade with molybdenum content, provide superior resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion in seawater applications.
```

---

## 示例（控制阀）

```markdown
## Control Valves are suitable for:

- **Process Control Systems**: Precise flow regulation is critical in chemical and petrochemical processes. Control valves automatically adjust flow rates based on process variables like temperature, pressure, and level.

- **Power Plants**: Steam flow control requires valves that can handle high temperatures and pressures while maintaining precise positioning. Control valves ensure efficient turbine operation and boiler feedwater regulation.

- **Water Distribution Networks**: Municipal water systems need valves that can modulate flow to maintain consistent pressure across the distribution network. Control valves help reduce water loss and optimize energy consumption.
```
