# SKILL-02: 开篇引言撰写

**用途**: 撰写文章前 2-3 段引言

---

## 结构模板

```
[第 1 段] 主题引入 + 行业地位描述（2-3 句）

[第 2 段] 背景信息 + 数据支撑 + 内部链接（2-3 句）

[第 3 段] 悬念引导 + 文章预告（1-2 句）
```

---

## 常用开篇句式

```
- "Among all the [产品类别] used in the [行业], [主题] is known for [特点]."
- "[主题] is manufactured to meet [标准]. Although [主题] isn't intended for [用途], its popularity is increasing in [应用领域]."
- "[主题] are essential components that have been used by [行业] for decades."
- "The [主题] and [主题 2] are two popular [类别] in the [行业]."
```

## 悬念引导句

```
- "Wondering which pressure class is right for your project? Read through the rest to find out everything you need to know about [主题]."
- "In this article, we'll review [主题], covering [内容 1], [内容 2], and more."
- "Read on to find out if it's the right choice for your pipeline project."
- "Read to the end to find out if it's a good investment for your industrial supply business."
```

---

## 示例（法兰类）

```markdown
Among all the different types of pipe components used in the oil and gas industry, flanges are known for their critical role in connecting pipelines. Class 150 and Class 300 flanges are the most commonly used pressure ratings in industrial applications.

When selecting flanges for your project, understanding the pressure-temperature ratings is essential. ASME B16.5 standard defines these ratings, ensuring safe operation under various conditions.

Wondering which flange class is right for your application? Read through the rest to find out everything you need to know about flange pressure classes, their differences, and how to choose the best option for your pipeline system.
```

## 示例（阀门类）

```markdown
Valves are the control points of any piping system, regulating the flow of fluids in industries ranging from water treatment to petrochemical plants. Among the various types, ball valves and gate valves are two of the most widely used solutions.

Both valve types serve different purposes and excel in specific applications. Understanding their differences can help you make informed procurement decisions for your projects.

In this article, we'll compare ball valves vs. gate valves, covering their working principles, advantages, limitations, and ideal use cases. Read on to find out which valve type is best suited for your needs.
```
