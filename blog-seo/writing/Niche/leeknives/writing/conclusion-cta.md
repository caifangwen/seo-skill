# SKILL-08 + SKILL-16: 结论与 CTA

**用途**: 生成文章结论总结（SKILL-16）和行动号召（SKILL-08）  
**适用**: 所有文章结尾

---

## SKILL-16: 购买建议总结

### 结构模板

```markdown
## Conclusion: which [主题] is better for [目标用户]

[总结主要差异]（1-2 句）.

[核心建议]（1-2 句）.

[价格说明]（可选）.

[最终建议 + CTA].
```

### 常用结论句式

```
- "The [特性] and [特性] are where [产品 A] proves to be better."
- "For most users, these upsides won't be a major deciding factor."
- "However, the improved [特性] can make a big difference for users using [场景]."
- "From a price point, there isn't a noticeable difference."
- "Overall, [主题] is a viable option for [用途]."
```

### 投资建议模板

```markdown
## Should you consider selling [主题]?

[主题] is popular among [用户群] due to [原因]. The high demand makes it a good choice for businesses.

If you are a business seeking advice on selling [主题], LeeKnives is the right place to begin. We offer wholesale solutions and expert advice. [CTA 链接].
```

### 示例（S30V vs S35VN）

```markdown
## Conclusion: which steel is better for your knife store

The toughness and the ability to be easily sharpened are where S35VN proves to be better steel. Since the edge retention and corrosion resistance are somewhat identical, for most users, these upsides won't be a major deciding factor.

However, the improved toughness and easier sharpening can make a big difference for users using a knife for work purposes.

From a price point, there isn't a noticeable difference. That said, most stores are better off choosing S35VN.

```
