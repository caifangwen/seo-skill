# SKILL-09: FAQ 问答生成

**用途**: 生成常见问题解答章节  
**适用**: 指南/科普文章、产品指南文章

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## Frequently Asked Questions

### 问题 1？

回答内容（2-3 句）...

### 问题 2？

回答内容（2-3 句）...

### 问题 3？

回答内容（2-3 句）...
```

---

## 问题类型

1. 定义类: "What is...?"
2. 对比类: "What's the difference between...?"
3. 使用类: "How do I...?"
4. 安全类: "Is... safe?"
5. 维护类: "How to care for...?"

---

## 回答结构

```
[直接回答]（1 句）

[补充说明]（1-2 句）

[相关建议/链接]（可选）
```

---

## 示例（Micarta）

```markdown
## FAQs

### What types of Micarta are commonly used for knife handles?

Two main types are canvas Micarta and paper Micarta. Canvas Micarta has a more textured appearance, while paper Micarta provides a smoother finish. Both offer durability, comfort, and good grip, but their choice depends on personal preferences and desired aesthetics.

### Is Micarta easy to maintain?

Micarta is relatively low-maintenance compared to other materials like metal or wood. It doesn't require any special care, as it is resistant to moisture and doesn't warp or crack. However, it may show scratches and wear over time, so it is recommended to clean and avoid rough use when possible.
```
