# SKILL-11: 优缺点分析

**用途**: 生成优缺点列表  
**适用**: 评测文章、对比文章、材料介绍文章

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
### Pros

- [优点 1]. [详细说明].

- [优点 2]. [详细说明].

- [优点 3]. [详细说明].

### Cons

- [缺点 1]. [详细说明].

- [缺点 2]. [详细说明].

- [缺点 3]. [详细说明].
```

---

## 常用句式

**优点开头**:
```
- "[主题] equips knives with excellent [优点]."
- "While [相关特性] strongly correlates with [另一特性], it also affects other areas."
- "[优点] isn't an issue for [主题]."
```

**缺点开头**:
```
- "The high [特性] doesn't provide easy [结果]."
- "The [特性] of [主题] is adequate for [场景]. However, users looking for [需求] may experience [问题]."
- "[主题] is a high-performance [类别]. You can expect a just as high price tag."
```

---

## 示例（M390）

```markdown
### Pros

- M390 steel equips knives with excellent edge retention. Even with daily, repeated use, it will get your customers further than many alternatives.

- While wear resistance strongly correlates with edge retention, it also affects other areas. The blades made from M390 steel last longer as they lose less material.

- Rusting or any form of corrosion isn't an issue for M390 steel knives. It does well in humid and wet settings.

### Cons

- The high hardness and wear resistance don't provide easy sharpening. M390 steel knives hold an edge but are difficult to sharpen.

- The toughness of M390 is adequate for everyday tasks. However, users looking for a robust blade to tackle high-impact applications may experience a chipped edge.

- M390 is a high-performance steel. You can expect a just as high price tag with its properties.
```
