# SKILL-02: 开篇引言撰写

**用途**: 撰写文章前 2-3 段引言

---

## 结构模板

```
[第 1 段] 主题引入 + 行业地位描述（2-3 句）

[第 2 段] 背景信息 + 数据支撑 + 内部链接（2-3 句）

[第 3 段] 悬念引导 + 文章预告（1-2 句）
```

---

## 常用开篇句式

```
- "Among all the [类别] used in the [行业], [主题] is known for [特点]."
- "[主题] is manufactured by [公司]. Although [主题] isn't intended for [用途], its popularity is increasing."
- "[主题] are traditional tools that have been used by [人群] for centuries."
- "The [主题] and [主题 2] are two popular [类别] in the [行业]."
```

## 悬念引导句

```
- "Wondering why? Read through the rest to find out everything you need to know about [主题]."
- "In this article, we'll review [主题], covering [内容 1], [内容 2], and more."
- "Read on to find out if it's a good match for your customers."
- "Read to the end to find out if it's a good investment for your knife business."
```

---

## 示例（钢材类）

```markdown
Among all the different types of steel used in the knife industry, some are known for a specific quality. ZDP-189 is powdered Japanese steel, most notably recognized for its exceptional hardness.

When thinking about hard steels, the general rating on the Rockwell hardness scale is anything 60 HRc and up. However, there aren't many names that come close to the hardness of ZDP-189 steel.

Wondering why? Read through the rest to find out everything you need to know about ZDP-189 steel, its properties, and what kind of knives can benefit from this steel the best.
```
