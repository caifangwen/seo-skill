# SKILL-12: 使用场景描述

**用途**: 描述产品适用场景  
**适用**: 所有文章类型

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## [主题] is suitable for:

- **[场景 1]**: [详细说明].

- **[场景 2]**: [详细说明].

- **[场景 3]**: [详细说明].
```

---

## 常用场景分类

| 场景 | 描述要点 |
|-----|---------|
| Kitchen knives | 专业厨师/家庭烹饪、切割精度、维护频率 |
| Outdoor knives | 露营/狩猎/生存、耐用性、多功能性 |
| EDC knives | 日常携带、便携性、实用性 |
| Tactical knives | 自卫/执法、强度、可靠性 |
| Collectible knives | 收藏价值、工艺、稀缺性 |

---

## 示例（ZDP-189）

```markdown
## ZDP-189 steel is suitable for:

- **Kitchen knives**: ZDP-189 steel is a favorable option for cooks preferring a long-lasting sharp edge, easy sharpening, and that don't mind the occasional maintenance for corrosion resistance.

- **Outdoor knives**: The knives with high wear resistance are favorable to any outdoor person. It plays an important role, from cutting ropes to slicing wood for a campfire.

- **Other applications**: ZDP-189 steel found its place in surgical scalpel blades and some machinery, but the primary use case is making high-end, custom knives.
```
