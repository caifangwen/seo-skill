# SKILL-14: 材料对比分析

**用途**: 对比不同材料特性  
**适用**: 材料介绍文章

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## Comparing [主题] with other [类别]

### [材料 A]

[材料 A 描述]. [与主题对比]. [优劣势].

### [材料 B]

[材料 B 描述]. [与主题对比]. [优劣势].

### [材料 C]

[材料 C 描述]. [与主题对比]. [优劣势].
```

---

## 对比维度

- 耐用性（Durability）
- 重量（Weight）
- 价格（Price）
- 维护难度（Maintenance）
- 外观（Aesthetics）
- 性能（Performance）

---

## 常用对比句式

```
- "When it comes to [维度], [材料 A] offers [特点]. However, compared to [主题], [差异]."
- "[材料 A] is known for [特点]. While both [主题] and [材料 A] are [共同点], [主题] tends to have [优势]."
- "Unlike [主题], [材料 A] [差异]."
```

---

## 示例（Micarta vs 其他）

```markdown
## Comparing Micarta with other knife handle materials

### Wood

When it comes to knife handles, natural wood offers a classic and visually appealing option. However, compared to Micarta, wood can be more susceptible to moisture and temperature fluctuations, which can cause it to warp or crack.

### G10

G10 is a very close alternative to Micarta. G10 is known for its rigidity and lightweight properties. While both Micarta and G10 are durable and resistant to moisture, Micarta tends to have a more comfortable grip due to its slightly softer nature.

### Ultem

Ultem is a high-performance thermoplastic material. Its main advantages stem from its exceptional heat resistance and strength. Unlike Micarta, Ultem handles do not develop patina and are significantly more expensive.
```
