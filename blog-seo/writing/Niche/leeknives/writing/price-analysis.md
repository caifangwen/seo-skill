# SKILL-15: 价格因素分析

**用途**: 分析产品价格因素  
**适用**: 价格分析文章、评测文章

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## Why is [主题] expensive?

[引入句]. Here's an overview of [因素 1] and [因素 2].

### [因素 1]

[详细说明]. [示例].

### [因素 2]

[详细说明]. [示例].

### [因素 3]

[详细说明]. [示例].
```

---

## 常见价格因素

| 因素 | 说明 |
|-----|------|
| 原材料成本 | 钢材等级、稀有材料 |
| 制造工艺 | 手工锻造、粉末冶金、热处理 |
| 品牌溢价 | 知名品牌、历史传承 |
| 稀缺性 | 限量生产、供应有限 |
| 文化价值 | 历史意义、收藏价值 |

---

## 示例（Damascus）

```markdown
## Why is pattern-forming crucible steel expensive?

The processes that involve making pattern-forming crucible steel are more extensive than any other steel. The manufacturing process can make even the powder metallurgy super steels simple to produce in comparison.

That said, it's a labor-intensive process. Besides, it relies on the expertise and knowledge of an expert artisan. There's also the cultural and historical significance of these steels.

While all these increase the price, there's also availability. It further drives the price higher. You will need strong connections in the industry to get your hands on any product made from this steel.
```
