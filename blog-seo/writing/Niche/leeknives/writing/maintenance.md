## 常见维护步骤

```
- 清洁: "After using, make sure to clean and dry thoroughly."
- 存放: "Store in a dry, cool place away from moisture."
- 使用注意: "Avoid using metal utensils / striking or prying."
- 磨刀: "Sharpen regularly using [工具]. Follow manufacturer's instructions."
- 防护: "Use a protective cover/sheath when not in use."
```

---

## 示例（Enameled Cast Iron）

```markdown
## How to care for enameled cast iron?

Keeping enameled cast iron cookware in good condition requires proper care and maintenance. Here are some tips:

1. **Use low to medium heat**: To avoid damaging the enamel, it's important to use low to medium heat. Higher heat can cause the enamel to crack or chip.

2. **Avoid using metal utensils**: Using metal utensils can scratch or damage the enamel coating. Utensils made of wood or silicone are the best choice.

3. **Hand wash with a soft sponge**: Use a soft sponge and mild detergent to hand wash. Do not use scouring pads and abrasive cleaners.

4. **Dry thoroughly**: After washing, be sure to dry the pan thoroughly to prevent rust from forming.
```
