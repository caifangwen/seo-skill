# SKILL-10: 历史背景叙述

**用途**: 撰写产品/材料的历史背景  
**适用**: 产品指南文章、材料介绍文章

---

## 结构模板

```markdown
## History of [主题]

[起源时间] + [起源地点/人群]（1 句）

[发展历程]（1-2 句）

[现代应用/地位]（1 句）
```

---

## 常用句式

```
- "[主题] have been used by [人群] of [地点] for centuries."
- "[主题] first appeared in [地点] during [时期], about [时间] ago."
- "Originally, it was designed for [原始用途]."
- "Over the centuries, the [主题] style spread beyond [起源] and was adopted by [新用户]."
```

---

## 示例（Tanto）

```markdown
### History

The tanto blade first appeared in Japan during the Heian period, about 1,000 years ago. Originally, it was designed for samurai to use as a small sword.

These blades became popular because they could pierce armor and were easy to carry. Over the centuries, the tanto style spread beyond Japan and was adopted by knife makers around the world.

Many modern tactical and survival knives use the tanto shape because of its proven strength and piercing power.
```
