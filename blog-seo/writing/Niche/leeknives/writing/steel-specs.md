# SKILL-03 + SKILL-04: 钢材成分与属性详解

**用途**: 生成钢材化学成分列表（SKILL-03）及各项性能详解（SKILL-04）  
**适用**: 钢材评测文章、钢材对比文章

---

## SKILL-03: 钢材成分列表

### 结构模板

```markdown
## [钢材名] steel composition

- **Carbon**: X%
- **Chromium**: X%
- **Vanadium**: X%
- **Molybdenum**: X%
- **Tungsten**: X%
- **Manganese**: X%
- **Silicon**: X%

Other than these, [钢材名] steel contains traces of [其他元素].
```

### 常见元素顺序（按重要性）

1. Carbon（碳）
2. Chromium（铬）
3. Vanadium（钒）
4. Molybdenum（钼）
5. Tungsten（钨）
6. Manganese（锰）
7. Silicon（硅）
8. 其他（Nickel, Cobalt, Niobium 等）

### 示例（M390）

```markdown
## M390 steel composition

- **Carbon**: 1.90%
- **Chromium**: 20.00%
- **Vanadium**: 4.00%
- **Molybdenum**: 1.00%
- **Silicon**: 0.70%
- **Tungsten**: 0.60%
- **Manganese**: 0.30%
```

## SKILL-04: 钢材属性详解

### 标准章节顺序

```markdown
## [钢材名] steel properties

### Hardness
### Corrosion resistance
### Edge retention
### Toughness
### Wear resistance
### Sharpening ease
```

### 每节结构

```markdown
### [属性名]

![相关图片](https://img.leeknives.com/wp-content/uploads/YYYY/MM/图片名.jpg)

[属性定义/说明]（1 句）

[具体数值/表现]（1-2 句）

[与同类对比]（1-2 句）

[内部链接]（1 句）

[使用建议]（1 句）
```

### 各属性常用表达

**硬度（Hardness）**:
```
- "The typical hardness of [钢材] is between X and Y HRc."
- "[钢材] can reach an average hardness of X HRc."
- "This degree of hardness is adequate for [用途]."
```

**耐腐蚀性（Corrosion resistance）**:
```
- "[钢材] performs [well/poorly] in corrosion resistance despite having [特点]."
- "The X% chromium enables incredible corrosion resistance."
- "Compared to [对比钢材], [钢材] has [better/worse] corrosion resistance."
```

**边缘保持性（Edge retention）**:
```
- "The X+ HRc hardness provides incredible edge retention."
- "[钢材] knives can stay sharp for long periods."
- "It performs better than most steels with [特点]."
```

**韧性（Toughness）**:
```
- "[钢材]'s toughness isn't as great as its other qualities."
- "Using a [钢材] knife vigorously can result in a chipped edge."
- "The toughness is adequate for everyday use."
```
