## 📊 Keganico 文章类型分类

基于现有 100+ 篇文章分析：

### 1. 钢材评测类 (Steel Reviews) - 30+ 篇

**代表文章**: 14C28N Steel, Nitro-V Steel, CruWear, MagnaCut, S30V, S35VN, M390, D2, VG-10

**标准结构**:
```
1. Front Matter
2. 开篇引入 (钢材定位 + 核心特点)
3. What is [Steel]? (定义 + 历史)
4. Chemical Composition (成分表)
5. Performance Analysis (性能分析)
6. [Steel] vs Other Steels (对比分析)
7. Best Uses & Limitations (使用场景)
8. FAQ (常见问题)
9. CTA (OEM/批发导向)
```

**核心技能**: [01](./writing/skills/01-front-matter.md), [02](./writing/skills/02-intro.md), [03](./writing/skills/03-steel-composition.md), [04](./writing/skills/04-steel-performance.md), [08](./writing/skills/08-cta.md), [09](./writing/skills/09-faq.md), [12](./writing/skills/12-use-cases.md), [13](./writing/skills/13-maintenance.md)
