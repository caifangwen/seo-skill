# SKILL-01: Front Matter 生成器

**用途**: 生成文章开头的 YAML Front Matter

---

## 输出格式

```yaml
---
title: "文章标题"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
categories:
  - "分类名称"
coverImage: "文章标题.jpg"
---
```

---

## Keganico 分类体系

| 分类名 | 适用内容 | 示例文章 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| `knife-basics` | 刀具基础知识 | What Is EDC Knife |
| `knife-steel` | 钢材评测/对比 | 14C28N Steel Review |
| `knife-market` | 品牌评测/市场 | Civivi Knives Review |
| `knife-materials` | 手柄/材料 | G-10 vs Micarta |
| `knife-manufacturing` | 制造/产地 | Where Are Benchmade Made |
| `oem-service` | OEM/B2B 服务 | OEM Knife Manufacturer |

---

## 标题规范

```
✅ 长度：40-70 字符
✅ 包含核心关键词
✅ 可使用问句/冒号
✅ 避免点击诱导

示例:
✅ "14C28N Steel: Underrated or Overhyped?"
✅ "MagnaCut vs. S30V: Which Steel is Best?"
✅ "What Is a Fixed Blade Knife and How to Choose One?"
❌ "You Won't Believe This Knife Secret!"
```
