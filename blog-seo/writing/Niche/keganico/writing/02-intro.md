# SKILL-02: 开篇引言撰写

**用途**: 撰写文章前 2-3 段引言

---

## Keganico 开篇公式

```
第 1 段 (80-120 字):
**粗体核心答案/定位** + 扩展说明 1-2 句

第 2 段 (80-150 字):
背景信息 + 数据支撑 + 内部链接

第 3 段 (可选，50-80 字):
内容预告 + 引导语
```

---

## 开篇类型模板

### 模板 1: 钢材评测开篇

```markdown
[钢材名] steel is often mistaken for [常见误解], but in reality, it [实际定位].
This article will help you understand whether it's worth choosing—especially if
you want [目标用户需求].

[钢材名] is a [钢材类型] developed by [公司/国家]. As an upgrade to [前代产品],
it achieves [性能提升] through [技术改进].

Originally co-designed with [合作品牌], it strikes a rare balance: [特点 1],
[特点 2], and [特点 3] — all at a [价格定位] price point.
```

**示例 (14C28N)**:
```markdown
14C28N steel is often mistaken for a "budget substitute," but in reality, it
outperforms many mid-to-high-end steels in several key parameters. This article
will help you understand whether it's worth choosing—especially if you want
reliable performance without breaking the bank.

14C28N is a high-performance stainless steel developed by Swedish steel giant
Sandvik (now Alleima) specifically for the knife industry.
```

### 模板 2: 对比文章开篇

```markdown
For decades, [A] was the industry benchmark for [领域]. However, as the market
moves toward [新趋势], [B] has disrupted this balance.

For [目标受众], the choice is now between [A 的优势] and [B 的优势].
```

**示例 (MagnaCut vs S30V)**:
```markdown
For decades, CPM S30V was the industry benchmark for premium knives. However,
as the market moves toward extreme corrosion resistance and high impact toughness,
CPM MagnaCut has disrupted this balance.

For sourcing managers, the choice is now between the proven, cost-effective edge
of S30V and the "indestructible" stainless profile of MagnaCut.
```

### 模板 3: 知识科普开篇

```markdown
**[粗体核心定义，50-70 字].** [扩展说明关键要素，1-2 句].

[第二段：说明为什么这个话题重要]. Whether you're [读者类型 1] or [读者类型 2],
understanding [主题] will help you [获得的好处].

In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore [内容 1], [内容 2], and [内容 3].
Read on to discover everything you need to know!
```

**示例 (Fixed Blade Knife)**:
```markdown
**A fixed blade knife is a cutting tool with a blade that is permanently attached
to the handle, making it extremely stable and reliable for a variety of tasks.**
Unlike folding knives, which have blades that can be tucked into the handle, the
blade of a fixed blade knife extends into the handle without any moving parts.

Owning a fixed blade knife means understanding its uses and how to maintain it.
These knives can be used for cutting, carving, slicing, and many other tasks
requiring a sharp, dependable edge.

In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the types of fixed blade knives, their
numerous uses, key components, and help you select the perfect one to suit your needs.
```

### 模板 4: 品牌评测开篇

```markdown
[品牌名] knives have become a major name in [市场定位] over the past [时间].
**These [产品特点] at prices that typically range from [价格区间].** If you're
[目标用户需求], understanding what makes [品牌] special is important for your business.

The brand emerged in [年份] as [品牌背景]. This connection gives [品牌优势].
```

**示例 (Civivi)**:
```markdown
Civivi knives have become a major name in the budget knife market over the past
five years. **These Chinese-made folding and fixed blade knives deliver premium
features like ceramic ball bearings, quality steel options, and solid construction
at prices that typically range from $60 to $80.** If you're looking to stock knives
that offer real value to your customers without breaking their budget, understanding
what makes Civivi special is important for your business.

The brand emerged in 2018 as a sister company to WE Knife Co. Ltd., one of China's
largest quality knife manufacturers.
```

## 常用引导句

```
悬念引导:
- "Wondering why? Read through the rest to find out..."
- "In this article, we'll review..., covering..., and more."
- "Read on to find out if it's a good match for your customers."

内容预告:
- "In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore..."
- "This article will help you understand..."
- "Let's dive into the details..."

B2B 导向:
- "For sourcing managers, the choice is now between..."
- "For [目标受众], understanding...is important for your business."
- "If you're looking to [商业目标], this guide will help..."
```
