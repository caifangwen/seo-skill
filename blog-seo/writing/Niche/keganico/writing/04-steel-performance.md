## 常用表达

### Toughness (韧性)

```markdown
[钢材名]'s most surprising feature is its [分数] toughness score. Its toughness
is [倍数] that of [对比钢材], even matching the toughness-renowned [参考钢材].
In practical applications, this exceptional toughness brings revolutionary advantages.

Manufacturers can confidently use sharp [角度] edge angles without worrying about
chipping. In contrast, [对比钢材] typically requires [角度] or more for strength.
```

### Corrosion Resistance (耐腐蚀性)

```markdown
The [分数] corrosion resistance score makes [钢材名] truly "stainless." The [铬含量]%
chromium content provides ample protection, with [其他元素] addition as the cherry on top.

Compared to [对比钢材]'s [分数] score, [钢材名]'s advantage is obvious. Many users
switching from [对比钢材] to [钢材名] mention the same thing: finally free from
rust worries.
```

### Edge Retention (边缘保持性)

```markdown
The [分数] edge retention score isn't outstanding, but needs contextual understanding.
In standardized CATRA testing (measuring total mm of material cut), [钢材名] performs
at approximately [百分比] of [对比钢材]'s level.

Interestingly, many users report [钢材名] feels "always sharp." The reason lies in
its extreme ease of sharpening—when the edge begins dulling, [时间] can restore it
sharper than new.
```

### Ease of Sharpening (易磨性)

```markdown
[钢材名]'s fine grain structure makes it one of the easiest quality steels to sharpen.
Using standard [目数] whetstones, even beginners can achieve razor sharpness in [时间].
In contrast, [对比钢材] typically requires diamond stones and [时间] of patience.

This ease of sharpening not only saves time but more importantly lowers the maintenance
barrier. Many users develop regular maintenance habits as a result.
```
