## Keganico FAQ 问题类型

### 1. 定义类

```
- What is [主题]?
- What does [术语] mean?
- What is the best [产品] for [用途]?
```

### 2. 对比类

```
- What's the difference between [A] and [B]?
- Is [A] better than [B]?
- Which is better for [用途]: [A] or [B]?
```

### 3. 使用类

```
- How do I [操作]?
- How to maintain [产品]?
- How long does [产品] last?
```

### 4. 安全/法律类

```
- Is [产品] legal to carry?
- Is [材料] safe for [用途]?
- Are there restrictions on [产品]?
```

### 5. 维护类

```
- How do I clean [产品]?
- How often should I [维护操作]?
- What should I avoid with [产品]?
```

## FAQ 示例 (钢材类)

```markdown
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is 14C28N steel good for knives?

Yes, 14C28N is an excellent choice for knives, especially in the mid-range price segment.
It offers outstanding toughness (9.0/10), good corrosion resistance (8.5/10), and extreme
ease of sharpening. While its edge retention isn't as good as premium steels like S30V,
the overall performance balance makes it ideal for EDC and kitchen knives.

### What is 14C28N steel equivalent to?

14C28N is most similar to Nitro-V and AEB-L. All three are fine-grained stainless steels
with nitrogen addition. 14C28N has slight advantages in toughness and corrosion resistance
compared to Nitro-V, while AEB-L is nearly identical in practice. 14C28N edges out both
in terms of supply chain maturity and heat treatment consistency.

### Is 14C28N better than D2?

For most applications, yes. 14C28N has 2.5x the toughness of D2 (9.0 vs 3.5) and far
superior corrosion resistance (8.5 vs 4.5). While D2 has slightly better edge retention,
14C28N is much easier to sharpen and nearly maintenance-free. Many D2 users switch to
14C28N primarily due to the rust resistance advantage.

### What HRC does 14C28N reach?

14C28N typically performs in the 58-61 HRC range with proper heat treatment. Some
manufacturers achieve up to 62 HRC with cryogenic treatment. This hardness range provides
the best balance of cutting performance and toughness. The steel is known for its
"forgiving" heat treatment, making it suitable for mass production.

### Is 14C28N stainless steel?

Yes, 14C28N is a true stainless steel. With 14% chromium content and 0.11% nitrogen
addition, it exceeds the 13% chromium threshold for stainless classification. Its
corrosion resistance score of 8.5/10 makes it nearly rust-proof in normal use conditions.
Many users report "finally free from rust worries" after switching from D2 or carbon steels.
```

## FAQ 写作规范

```
✅ 每个问题:
- 2-4 句回答
- 直接回答问题
- 包含具体数据/分数
- 可包含内部链接
- 使用粗体强调关键信息

✅ 问题选择:
- 3-6 个问题为宜
- 覆盖用户最关心的话题
- 包含对比类问题
- 包含使用/维护问题

❌ 避免:
- 过长回答 (>150 字)
- 绕弯子不直接回答
- 缺少数据支撑
- 过于技术化
```
