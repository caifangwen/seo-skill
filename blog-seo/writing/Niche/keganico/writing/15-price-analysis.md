## 示例 (MagnaCut vs S30V)

```markdown
## Cost Analysis & Market Pricing

| **Steel** | **Target MSRP** | **Market Strategy** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **CPM S30V** | $120–$200 | Best for "Premium Value" lines where the name carries weight but budget is key. |
| **CPM MagnaCut** | $240–$450+ | Reserved for high-margin flagship models with extreme performance claims. |

### Factors Affecting Price

- **Material Cost**: MagnaCut powder steel is significantly more expensive than
  conventional S30V due to the complex powder metallurgy process.

- **Manufacturing Complexity**: MagnaCut requires precise heat treatment and
  specialized grinding equipment.

- **Brand Premium**: Established brands command higher prices for MagnaCut offerings.

- **Market Demand**: As the "steel of the year," MagnaCut commands premium pricing.

### Value Assessment

For serious knife enthusiasts and professionals, MagnaCut offers excellent value
through its long-term performance and low maintenance. However, casual users may
find S30V provides better value for everyday tasks at half the price.

### Investment Perspective

MagnaCut knives from reputable manufacturers tend to hold their value well in the
secondary market, making them a reasonable investment for collectors. S30V remains
the safe choice for mass-market production with proven demand.
```
